Our Mission

Our aim as a company is to educate kids ranging between the year groups P5 and P7, the importance of global warming and the impact our actions have on the planet everyday.

Global warming or Climate Change refers to the average long-term changes over the entire Earth. These include warming temperatures and changes in precipitation, as well as the effects humans have on the Earth, such as:

The Comic

Our monthly comic strips are designed to educate young children on how their actions have a affect on the world around us. The first of the collection is based around the rise in plastic that ends up in our oceans.

A simple story of two kids, Sarah and Jamie, who decide to treat themselves at the beach one sunny afternoon. Little did they know one small action could have a domino effect on the creatures who inhabit at the seaside!

The moral of this comic strip is to teach our children to not be lazy and put their rubbish in the bin.

The Global Warning, a fun way for parents, teachers or family members to spend time together and educate our ever growing youth at the same time!
